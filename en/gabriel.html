GABRIEL RUD
Orbiting
VIDEO INSTALLATION. HD VIDEO WITH NO SOUND, LOOP
A body, a digital sculpture shaped by Rud in an intense morphological search. Its rotative movement is infinite and gradually displays the different sides of the body within the mystery of its own shape, of its unattainable interior and the astonishment that hinders the recognition of its surface. The name of the piece also suggests that besides rotation there is also a journey that modifies its relationship with space and the observer, in a double game between the possibility of its physical existence and the plastic process per se.
Gabriel Rud (Argentina, 1979) is a logic and form technique specialist, as well as an image and sound designer graduated at the Universidad de Buenos Aires. There he works as Assistant Professor of Audiovisual Design, Campos Trilnick Chair, and is a Researcher at proyectoidis.org (Research in Image and Sound Design). He was awarded the 1st prize of the National Hall, New Media and Installations category. His works have been exhibited in galleries, halls, museums and international festivals.
