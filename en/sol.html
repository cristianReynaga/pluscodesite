SOL DEL RIO
Luminar
FULL HD VIDEO WITH STEREO SOUND. LENGTH: 3:00
Taking a video register of an electric storm, del Río proposes an aesthetic cutting of the phenomenon, whereby she constructs a system of rules that enable the component pieces of the image of the storm to conform a new order. The aesthetic information of the natural phenomenon is captured and afterwards subject to a certain industrial logic, where the artist’s perception intermingles with the software languages used in her investigation. At first sight, the result is a geometric abstraction where the light effect has a leading role: at a deeper level, a sort of mechanism among the levels of the video: It works to compose and decompose the light trapped in the cycle of the fountain.
Sol del Río (Argentina, 1981) investigates interface as a visual landscape, the spatial-temporal simultaneity and the intersection nature-technology. She utilized different resources and softwares, working with sound, video, animation, paint and performance. Among her exhibits: MonsterShine, Live A/V, Mutek SAT Montreal CAN 2011, Resonancia, album y Live A/V, Artlab Cúpula CCK BA Arg 2016.
