MOTUS.LUMINA
Urban Minor
GENERATIVE AUDIOVISUAL INSTALLATION
Urban Minor is an audio-visual generative installation where the artists present their view on life in the great global city we live in. Big cities may differ from one another in their architecture, their history and their culture, but the melancholy and repeatability underlying their colours and noises are present in all of them.. In the investigation process, artists fuse this urban theme with the studies on sound and colour developed by the English theorist D.D. Jamson (19th century). The fusion is handled by Motus Lumina in a minor repetitive style to create Urban Minor
